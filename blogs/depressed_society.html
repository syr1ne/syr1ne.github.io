depressed society


"I am living in a depressed society."

I say that because whenever I look around, I only find that every single person is depressed in some way. Someone is depressed about craving a better life; someone is depressed about getting a higher-salary job, a better family, a better relationship, or a better personality.

When was the last time you saw a person who had everything they wanted?... and is [s]he really happy now after getting all they want?

Humans are the smartest and, at the same time, stupidest animals. Dogs woke up, ate, drank, pooped, mated, walked, and slept. Cats do the same, and even most animals do the same. They don't care about whether they will get food tomorrow or not. They just live in the present. but we are not. always in either the past or future. craving and starving. hating and liking.

And when we start dealing with problems in our lives, we don't face them; rather, we want to forget them, choosing some kind of drug, which makes our lives even more worse.

I don't think life is as bad as we imagine it is; I think it's not. and if it is, there is no point in thinking about it. because most of the sadness we get is not from what is happening to us but from what we imagine could happen to us.
