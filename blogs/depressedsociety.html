Depressed Society


"i am living in a depressed society"

Do you know why I felt that way?

One day, I watched a movie named “300”. I got to know a lot about spartans, and the thing that highly inspired me is their mindset. They are legends.

True Human.

I would say that spartans don't lie in a category of depressed society because those guys are tough, not only from the outside but also from the inside. There is a huge difference between them and us Those people jump over bulls, we didn’t even have courage to do that. They are so disciplined that they don’t even think of just skipping a day from their workout whereas this is our first thought as soon as our alarm rings.

So what is the depressing part here?

Clearly, it’s the way we think. We spend most of our time thinking “how can i overcome procrastination?”, “Does skipping a day from exercise makes any difference?” and this keeps going on and on…

Spartans never think about such things, never. It is because they were taught that way. They were never taught to have choices, choice of skipping things, choice of running from stuff.

Look at today's man, they think smoking and consuming alcohol makes them men. Women think having a good body shape makes them women. I only saw a feared person, fear of not acceptance in society, fear of responsibilities, fear of losing. They try to run from it by consuming some kind of drugs which gives them short term pleasure.

Whereas spartans who are only “300” men gone to war, fought 100,000 soldiers, and saved their wives and children, following their responsibility of being a man, being a father, being a husband, not letting a single problem reach to their family.

In my opinion, that's what men and women are, who are strong enough to handle any situation and tough time with full courage, training their kids to be a strong warrior and at the end, left being an inspiration for them.
